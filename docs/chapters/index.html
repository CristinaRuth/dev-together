We expect our Chapter Leaders to be inclusive, welcoming people with strong verbal and written communication skills. Chapter Leaders should be technical, and be able to effectively manage tasks and time. This role is flexible, but does require a commitment of 10-20 hours per month.

All Chapter Leaders will join a group of equally passionate individuals and will receive the support of the full Dev Together organization.
