Who we are

Dev Together is a community focused on connecting poeple starting their development careers with technical mentors through low-commitment, high-value, human interactions.

The number of people learning software development increases each year, but the entry-level job market is not increasing at the same rate as the education boom. Bootcamp graduation rates have increased 20.4% from 2017-2018, while 4-year CS degrees were awarded at a rate of 17.3% more in 2017 than 2016.

In contrast, the increasingly complex technology landscape coupled with companies reluctant to invest in junior talent has resulted in a job market that demands a high level of proficiency in a broad range of skills from its entry-level talent.
